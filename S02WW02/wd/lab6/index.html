Welcome to Cork Donkey Sanctuary

Cork Donkey Sanctuary is a no-kill donkey rescue and adoption service that works tirelessly to help rescued donkeys find loving adoptive families.

Do you have any unwanted donkeys? Maybe you've a Christmas gift that has outgrown its paddock. Or a beast of burden that has become a burdensome beast. Don't chop your unwelcome donkey into a sack and dump it into the nearest supermarket carpark! We can offer your donkey a loving home.

Ralph

Despite appearances, Ralph is a cantankerous donkey, whom we would be pleased to turn into glue.

Only kidding, Ralph!

Ralph will make an attempt on a world record this year. He hopes to set a new record for the fastest time to eat a banana with no hands. (Luckily for Ralph, he has no hands.) According to the Guinness Book of Records, the current record is 17.82 seconds. If you would like to sponsor Ralph's attempt on the record, then phone +353 21 1122334.
