CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

EN-003, designated Ephemeral Nexus, is to be stored within a standard containment chamber at Site-███,
previously housed at University College Cork, Ireland. Access to the chamber requires Level 3 clearance,
and all personnel entering must undergo psychological evaluation before and after interaction with
Ephemeral Nexus. The containment chamber must be equipped with a two-way communication system for
monitoring purposes.

Any experimentation involving Ephemeral Nexus requires written approval from Site Director Dr. ██████.
In the event of unexpected behavior or anomalous activity, personnel are instructed to immediately
vacate the containment chamber and report the incident to Site Security.

DESCRIPTION

Ephemeral Nexus (EN-003) was discovered on [DATA EXPUNGED] in the ████████ Forest, Germany, following
reports of unusual weather patterns and electromagnetic disturbances in the area. Ephemeral Nexus is a
humanoid entity measuring approximately 1.8 meters in height. Its appearance is obscured by a thick
layer of an unknown, viscous substance, preventing direct visual confirmation of its features.

The anomalous properties of Ephemeral Nexus manifest when any sentient being enters its proximity,
triggering a cognitohazardous effect. Individuals within a 10-meter radius of Ephemeral Nexus experience
vivid hallucinations, typically involving their deepest fears and regrets. Extended exposure to
Ephemeral Nexus results in severe psychological trauma, leading to symptoms such as insomnia, paranoia,
and suicidal tendencies.

Efforts to communicate with Ephemeral Nexus have been unsuccessful. Attempts to analyze the substance
covering its form have proven challenging, as any samples collected evaporate upon removal from the
entity.

Experiment Log - EN-001

Experiment Title: Memory Enhancement Testing

Objective: To assess the extent of EN-001's memory enhancement capabilities on human subjects.

Procedure: D-19234, a Class D personnel, equipped with a recording device and remotely monitored by research personnel, entered Ephemeral Nexus's containment chamber.

Results :

D-19234 began experiencing hallucinations within 30 seconds of entering the chamber. Hallucinations included manifestations of personal traumas and fears. Despite being instructed to leave the chamber after five minutes, D-19234 refused, displaying signs of distress and anxiety.

After 10 minutes, D-19234 was evacuated from the chamber. Subsequent psychological evaluations revealed severe trauma, and D-19234 was transferred to a psychiatric facility for further observation and treatment.

Addendum: As of [DATA EXPUNGED], Ephemeral Nexus has displayed no further anomalous activity. All cognitohazardous effects appear to have ceased. The entity is considered neutralized, and containment procedures have been adjusted accordingly. Research is ongoing to understand the nature of the entity's neutralization and to determine if any latent effects persist. The entity was transferred to Site-███ after previously being housed at University College Cork, Ireland.
