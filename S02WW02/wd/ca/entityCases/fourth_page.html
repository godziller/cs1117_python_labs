CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

EN-004, known as the Whispering Mirror, is to be housed in a standard secure locker at Site-██. Access
to the locker is restricted to Level 2 personnel, and any experimentation requires approval from the
Site Supervisor. The locker is to be checked monthly for any signs of wear or damage.

Personnel interacting with EN-004 should be made aware of its potential psychological effects, and
extended exposure is discouraged. If any unusual occurrences or heightened emotional states are
reported, personnel are to cease interaction immediately, and a psychological evaluation is to be
conducted.

DESCRIPTION

The Whispering Mirror (EN-004) is a vintage, ornate mirror with an intricately designed wooden frame.
The glass itself is slightly warped, giving a distorted reflection. When a person gazes into the mirror
for an extended period, they report hearing faint whispers, often resembling hushed conversations or
indistinct voices.

The whispers are unique to each observer, seemingly addressing personal fears, desires, or unresolved
issues. Subjects have reported a heightened emotional response and occasional feelings of unease after
interacting with EN-004. The mirror does not display any anomalous properties in terms of physical
reflection.

Efforts to communicate with the source of the whispers have been unsuccessful. No discernible origin or
entity has been identified within the mirror.

Experiment Log - EN-004-01:

Experiment Title: Memory Enhancement Testing

Objective: To observe the effects of prolonged exposure to Whispering Mirror.

Procedure: D-67890, equipped with a voice recorder, was instructed to gaze into EN-004 for 10 minutes.

Results :

D-67890 reported hearing whispers that seemed to reference past traumas and personal regrets.
Post-experiment, D-67890 exhibited signs of emotional distress, including increased anxiety. Analysis of
the voice recording revealed indistinct murmurs and whispers, though no clear origin could be
determined.

Addendum: EN-004-A Due to the unpredictable nature of the emotional impact reported by individuals interacting with the
Whispering Mirror, caution is advised. Regular monitoring of personnel who have interacted with EN-004
is recommended, and any significant emotional disturbances should be promptly addressed through
counseling and support services.
