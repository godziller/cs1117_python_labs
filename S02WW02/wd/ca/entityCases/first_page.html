:Entity 001:

  • NICKNAME: The Memory Lens
  • OBJECT CLASS: SAFE
  • DANGER RISK: SAFE

CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES

EN-001 is to be stored in a standard containment locker at Site-19. Access to EN-001 requires Level
2 clearance, and all testing must be approved by the current Site Director. Personnel handling
EN-001 are to undergo routine psychological evaluations to monitor for any unintended effects on
mental well-being. In the event of misuse or unauthorized access, appropriate disciplinary action
will be taken.

DESCRIPTION

EN-001 is a pair of non-anomalous eyeglasses resembling mid-20th-century prescription spectacles. The
frames are made of tortoiseshell-patterned plastic, and the lenses are clear glass. No markings or
brand names are present on the glasses, and attempts to trace their origin have been unsuccessful.

When a person wears EN-001, they experience a heightened ability to recall and vividly visualize
memories. The glasses seem to enhance the wearer's memory retrieval processes, allowing them to
remember details with exceptional clarity. Subjects report that memories appear more vibrant, with
increased sensory details such as colors, sounds, and scents.

Testing has shown that EN-001 does not alter or manipulate
the actual memories themselves but enhances the individual's
capacity to access and relive them. The glasses have been observed
to work on memories of various ages and types, including long-term
and short-term memories.

EN-001 was discovered in the possession of a retired librarian,
who reported the glasses' ability to improve her recall of
historical events and archival information. The Foundation
became aware of EN-001 after reports of the librarian accurately
recalling minute details from her past, including
specific dates, conversations, and even unrelated events.

Due to its benign nature and lack of any harmful effects, EN-001 has been classified as Safe.
However, access to EN-001 is restricted to authorized personnel for research purposes only.
Experimentation is ongoing to determine the full extent of EN-001's capabilities and potential
applications in aiding memory-related disorders.

Experiment Log - EN-001

Experiment Title: Memory Enhancement Testing

Objective: To assess the extent of EN-001's memory enhancement capabilities on human subjects.

Procedure: A controlled experiment will be conducted with a group of ten D-Class personnel. Each
subject will be provided with EN-001 and asked to recall specific details from a series of standardized
memory tests. The tests will include recalling lists of words, numbers, and images presented to the
subjects. The control group will undergo the same tests without wearing EN-001.

Variables:

  1. Independent Variable: Wearing EN-001.
  2. Dependent Variables:
    1. Accuracy of memory recall.
    2. Speed of memory retrieval.
    3. Subjective experience of enhanced memory.

Experiment Timeline:

  1. Pre-Experiment: All subjects will undergo a baseline memory assessment to establish a control for comparison.
  2. Phase 1: Subjects wearing EN-001 will undergo memory tests, while the control group will undergo the same tests without wearing EN-001.
  3. Interim Assessment: Subjects will be asked to provide feedback on their experience wearing EN-001, focusing on any notable changes in memory perception.
  4. Phase 2: Subjects will switch conditions (EN-001 wearers become the control group, and vice versa) to eliminate potential bias.
  5. Post-Experiment: A final memory assessment will be conducted on both groups to measure any lasting effects of wearing EN-001.

Data Collection

  1. Record accuracy and speed of memory recall for each subject.
  2. Document subjective feedback regarding the experience of wearing EN-001.
  3. Monitor for any adverse psychological effects during and after the experiment.

Ethical Considerations:

  1. Informed consent will be obtained from all D-Class personnel participating in the experiment.
  2. Psychological support and counseling will be made available to subjects before and after the experiment.

Note: This experiment aims to better understand the practical applications and limitations of EN-001's memory enhancement capabilities. Results will inform future research and potential uses of EN-001 in memory-related disorders or cognitive enhancement.
