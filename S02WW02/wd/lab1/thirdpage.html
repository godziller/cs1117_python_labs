Kendrick Lamar

In contrast to Radiohead, Kendick Lamar is HipHop. Kendricks music is also deeply poetical, often to be regarded as sometimes TOO
thought provoking for every day consumption. Personally I disagree, he has got some absolute Bumpers
Many regard Kendrick to be the best rapper ever, I agree. Ive listened to allot of hiphop and rap and am yet to find a rapper whose
music ticks all boxes, those being.

  • Good lyrics (Bars)
  • A good beat -> this being one thats original, creative, and interesting
  • Not Boring

I used to be moderately into his music until i went to see him live. I can whole heartedly say that it was the best concert ive
ever been to and ive been to a good few. My favorite album by Kendrick is probably "How To Pimp A Butterfly" due to its jazz and funk
influences, the Album itself is a 10 and i rarely give that out to any album.
